# Let the Pigeon Drive the Bus

New York City-inspired Godot platformer game made for Parthenon. Inspired by the *Don't Let the Pigeon Drive the Bus!* book series. There are two levels that are in order of increasing difficulty.

## How to Play

Use WASD or the arrow keys to move up, left, down, and right. Use W or the space key to jump.

## Goal of the Game

Collect the cookies to earn energy and make it to the end of level 2! If the pigeon survives, he will be able to drive the bus.
